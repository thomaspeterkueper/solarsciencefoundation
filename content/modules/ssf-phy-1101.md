# What is gravity?

Gravity is the attraction between masses. Every object with mass contributes to gravity, but the effect becomes obvious when masses are large: planets, moons, stars and spacecraft.

On Earth, gravity makes objects fall toward the ground. In space, gravity bends motion into orbits. A satellite does not simply hover; it falls around Earth, the Moon or another body while moving sideways fast enough to keep missing the surface.

For NOXIA, gravity is the first gateway concept for orbital navigation. A player who understands gravity can begin to understand why routes, stations, fuel use and planetary transfer windows matter.

## Learning goals

- Explain gravity as attraction between masses.
- Understand why falling and orbiting belong together.
- Connect gravity to basic space navigation.
